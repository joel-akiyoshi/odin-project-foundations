Ground Turkey Meatloaf

Description

This ground turkey meatloaf is juicy, flavorful, and topped with a sweet-savory glaze of ketchup, mustard, and brown sugar. It’s lighter than beef meatloaf but still full of rich comfort-food flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground turkey
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (plain or Italian)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)

Glaze

  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a loaf pan or baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, chopped onion, minced garlic, eggs, breadcrumbs, milk, salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix gently until just combined — don’t overmix.
  3. Form the mixture into a loaf shape and place it in the prepared pan.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard. Spread half of the glaze over the top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from oven, spread the remaining glaze on top, and bake another 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Let the meatloaf rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with mashed potatoes or vegetables.

Tips

  • For a bit of heat, add a dash of hot sauce or smoked paprika to the glaze.
  • Ground turkey can be lean—don’t skip the milk or glaze, as they help keep it moist.
